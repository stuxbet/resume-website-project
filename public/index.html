Hi. My name is Alexa Summers. I am an Assistant Professor of Video Game Development at Johnson County Community College, and an adjunct in the School of Computing and Engineering at the University of Missouri—Kansas City. I have both my bachelor's and master's degrees in Computer Science, and am working towards a Graduate Certificate in College Teaching and Career Preparation through the UMKC Preparing Future Faculty Program. My past research efforts involve atmospheric science research at Tel Aviv University in Israel, with a focus on natural disaster-predictions, and data science research at University of Missouri—Kansas City, with a focus on anxiety detection in dementia patients. My professional goals include implementing new curricula across both institutions involving community-based collaborations, as well as becoming a department chair.
